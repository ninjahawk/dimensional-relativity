# What Determines Dimension? — Determinants, Thresholds, and Constraints

**The narrowed question (yours):** At universal scale, does the *net gravity* of
an enormous mass concentration — a giant supercluster versus a lone galaxy — set
the **effective dimension** of that region? Is there a **threshold**? And what,
fundamentally, is dimension a function of?

This document answers it falsifiably: every candidate determinant gets a
mechanism, a law, a threshold, the current empirical constraint, and the
measurement that would kill it.

---

## 0. First, four distinct meanings of "dimension"

The answer is different for each, and conflating them is the entire source of
confusion. "Dimension is the way things are organized at a fundamental layer"
(your words) points specifically at #3 and #4.

| # | Name | What it counts | Can it vary? |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Topological / force-law | independent directions; sets `F ∝ 1/r^(D−1)` | No (locally) — pinned at 3 |
| 2 | Geometric / fractal | how matter fills space | **Yes** — non-integer, local |
| 3 | Spectral | how diffusion/fields propagate | **Yes** — runs with scale |
| 4 | Holographic | # of fundamental degrees of freedom | **Yes** — gravity controls it |

---

## 1. Determinant: CURVATURE (General Relativity) — *the null result*

- **Mechanism:** mass-energy curves spacetime via `G_μν = 8πG T_μν`.
- **Effect on dimension:** **none.** Curving a manifold does not change its
  dimension. A supercluster, a star, a black-hole exterior — all remain locally
  3+1 D. Gravity bends space; it does not add or remove directions.
- **Threshold:** n/a.
- **Constraint:** the inverse-square law (D=3) is verified from **<0.1 mm**
  (Eöt-Wash torsion balance) to **galaxy-cluster and cosmological** scales. No
  deviation. This is why "supercluster gravity makes space 4D" is excluded for
  meaning #1.
- **Falsify:** detect `F ∝ 1/r^(D−1)` with D≠3 at any scale. None seen.

**Verdict:** for the *force-law* dimension, net gravity does nothing. If your
idea is about #1, the data already says no.

---

## 2. Determinant: GRAVITATIONAL COMPACTNESS (Holography) — *the real threshold*

This is the rigorous home of your idea, because holographic entropy literally
counts the *fundamental degrees of freedom* (meaning #4) and **gravity sets it.**

- **Mechanism:** the maximum entropy in a region is not extensive (`∝ volume`,
  3D) but the Bekenstein–Hawking **area law** `S = A / 4 l_p²` (`∝ area`, 2D).
  Ordinary matter is volume-organized; strong gravity forces area-organization.
- **Law / control parameter:** compactness `ζ = R_s/R = 2GM/(Rc²)`.
  Effective dof-dimension `d_dof = d ln S_max/d ln R`: **3 for ζ≪1 → 2 as ζ→1.**
- **Threshold:** **ζ = 1**, the horizon. (Computed, Exp 5.)
- **Where real objects sit (Exp 5, real numbers):**

  | object | ζ = R_s/R | regime |
  |---|---|---|
  | Milky Way | 7×10⁻⁷ | 3D |
  | Coma cluster | 3×10⁻⁵ | 3D |
  | Laniakea supercluster | 1×10⁻⁴ | 3D |
  | black hole | 1.0 | **2D (threshold)** |
  | **observable universe** | **1.0000 (exact)** | **2D (threshold)** |

- **Exact result:** a flat (critical-density) universe satisfies `R_s = R_H`
  identically → **the universe as a whole always sits exactly at the holographic
  threshold.** Derivation in Exp 5; not a fit.
- **Constraint / status:** holography is mainstream (black-hole thermodynamics,
  AdS/CFT). Its cosmological application (holographic/Barrow entropy of the
  horizon) is active research.
- **Falsify:** show black-hole entropy scales as volume not area (it doesn't —
  Hawking), or that horizon thermodynamics fails.

**Verdict — direct answer to your question:** Yes, net gravity sets the
fundamental (holographic) dimension, with a sharp threshold at ζ=1. **But a
supercluster is ~10⁴× too diffuse to reach it.** Only black holes and the whole
observable universe do. So the effect you intuited is real and cosmological, but
it does **not** distinguish a big cluster from a small one — both are at ζ≈0.
The threshold is the horizon, full stop.

## 3. Determinant: ENERGY / PROBE SCALE (Spectral flow) — *the early universe*

- **Mechanism:** in every major quantum-gravity program the **spectral dimension
  runs**: `d_s → 2` at short distance / high energy, `→ 4` at large scale.
- **Threshold:** the **Planck scale** (`l_p ≈ 1.6×10⁻³⁵ m`, `E ≈ 10¹⁹ GeV`).
- **Cosmological reading (Exp 7, frontier hypothesis):** near the singularity
  (Planck density) the effective dimension was ~2, "unfolding" toward 3+1 as the
  universe expanded and cooled. This is your singularity-expansion picture, and
  it is a real published idea (evolving/vanishing dimensions).
- **Constraint / status:** the UV flow `d_s→2` is robust across **four unrelated
  programs** (CDT, asymptotic safety, Hořava gravity, loop quantum gravity) —
  the strongest current support that dimension is dynamical. The cosmological
  version is speculative.
- **Falsify:** (a) a smooth primordial gravitational-wave spectrum through the
  predicted high-frequency cutoff; (b) perfect isotropy of ultra-high-energy
  cosmic rays at all energies.

**Verdict:** dimension is genuinely a function of energy density / scale, with
the threshold at the Planck scale — again pointing **down** to 2 at high density.

---

## 4. Determinant: ENERGY DENSITY opening EXTRA dimensions — *the only "up" route*

The single framework where high concentration gives **more** than 3 dimensions:

- **Mechanism:** large/warped extra dimensions (ADD, Randall–Sundrum). Gravity
  propagates in 3+n spatial dimensions; we are confined to a 3-brane. Above a
  threshold energy, gravity "sees" the extra dimensions and strengthens.
- **Threshold:** model-dependent, as low as ~**TeV** in original ADD.
- **Constraint:** **LHC** searches (missing energy, no microscopic black holes,
  dijet/dilepton) exclude the simplest versions up to **several TeV**; sub-mm
  gravity tests bound extra-dimension size to **< ~30 μm**. No evidence found.
- **Falsify:** already being falsified piecewise — each null result pushes the
  threshold higher.

**Verdict:** the "density → higher dimension" route exists in principle but is
empirically squeezed; nothing yet supports it, and it triggers at the TeV
(microscopic/high-energy) scale, not the supercluster (macroscopic) scale.

## 5. Bonus: AdS/CFT — matter literally *generating* a dimension

In gauge/gravity duality a strongly-interacting field theory in `d` dimensions
is **exactly equivalent** to gravity in `d+1` dimensions; the extra dimension is
the **energy/renormalization scale** of the matter. This is the deepest real
sense in which "matter creates a dimension" — but the emergent dimension is the
holographic/scale direction, not a new place to walk. It is the closest physics
comes to your original intuition, and it is rigorous.

## 6. Synthesis — what dimension is actually determined by

1. **Force-law dimension (#1):** fixed at 3 by orbital/atomic stability
   (Ehrenfest; Exp 4). Not touched by mass concentration. Settled.
2. **Geometric & spectral & holographic dimensions (#2–4):** genuinely variable,
   non-integer, and **set by the matter/energy distribution and by gravity** —
   exactly your thesis. Confirmed by simulation (Exp 2, 6) and by mainstream
   theory (holography, spectral flow).
3. **Direction of the effect (the one correction to your guess):** across *every*
   rigorous handle — cosmic web (Exp 2), mass-contrast sweep (Exp 6), holography
   (Exp 5), spectral flow (Exp 7) — **more mass-energy concentration drives the
   effective dimension DOWN toward 2, not up.** The only "up" mechanism (extra
   dimensions) is constrained away to ≥ TeV.
4. **The threshold you asked for is real and singular:** `ζ = R_s/R = 1`, the
   **horizon**. Reached only by black holes and by the universe as a whole.

## 7. What is genuinely still open (honest)

- **Why 3+1 macroscopically?** No first-principles derivation. Anthropic
  (stability, Exp 4) is a constraint, not an explanation.
- **Is dimension fundamental or emergent?** Unknown. CDT/AdS-CFT suggest
  emergent; we don't know what it emerges *from*.
- **What exactly fixes the IR value at 4 (not 3 or 5)?** Open.

These are real frontier questions. Your instinct — that dimension is *determined
by something physical, not a given* — is on the right side of them. The physical
"something" is energy density and gravity, the quantitative tool is entropy/dof
scaling, and the measured direction is reduction toward 2 at high concentration.

- Run the `D = 3 − γ` / `D_loc`-vs-density analysis on **real SDSS/DESI** data;
